<p><strong>The Wireline Logging Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cased Hole Wireline Logging Services</li><li>Open Hole Wireline Logging Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireline logging services market is categorized into two primary types: cased hole and open hole logging services. Cased hole wireline logging involves measurements conducted in wells with installed casing, enabling evaluation of production zones and integrity assessments. Conversely, open hole wireline logging is performed in uncased boreholes, allowing for direct analysis of the geological formations and fluid characteristics before casing installation. Both services are essential for optimizing oil and gas exploration and production by providing critical subsurface information.</p></p>
