<p><strong>The Fired Air Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fired</li><li>Indirect Fired</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fired air heater market is categorized into direct fired and indirect fired systems. Direct fired heaters use combustion to heat air directly, resulting in immediate temperature rise and efficiency but may introduce contaminants into the air. In contrast, indirect fired heaters use a heat exchanger to transfer heat from combustion gases to the air, ensuring cleaner output but typically at a slightly lower efficiency. Both types are utilized across various industries based on specific heating needs and operational conditions.</p></p>
