<p><ul><li>Dry-SP</li><li>Wet-SP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sandpaper market is divided into two primary types: dry sandpaper (Dry-SP) and wet sandpaper (Wet-SP). Dry-SP is designed for use in dry conditions, typically for general sanding tasks on wood, metal, and other surfaces. It offers a range of grits for various applications. In contrast, Wet-SP is used with water or other lubricants, reducing dust and preventing clogging, making it ideal for finishing tasks on wood and automotive surfaces. Both types cater to specific sanding needs based on their applications.</p></p>
