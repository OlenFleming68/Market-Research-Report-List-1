<p><p>The Floating Dock Cranes market comprises two primary types: Single Boom and Double Boom Floating Dock Cranes. Single Boom Floating Dock Cranes are designed for efficient lifting and relocation of heavy loads with a single arm, making them ideal for smaller operations and limited space. In contrast, Double Boom Floating Dock Cranes feature two arms for increased lifting capacity and stability, suitable for larger vessels and more demanding tasks. Both types offer flexibility and mobility for marine construction, repair, and logistics.</p></p>
