<p><p>The adhesive coatings market includes various types based on their composition and application. Non-Reactive Adhesives form strong bonds without undergoing a chemical reaction. Solvent-Based Adhesives contain organic solvents that evaporate, leaving a strong bond. Water-Based Dispersion Adhesives use water as a primary solvent, offering low toxicity and easy cleanup. Other types encompass specialized or hybrid formulations catering to specific industry needs. Collectively, these adhesives serve diverse applications across automotive, construction, packaging, and consumer products sectors.</p></p>
