<p><p>The self-adhesive labels market encompasses various printing techniques, with flexographic and letterpress printing being prominent methods. Flexographic printing utilizes flexible relief plates to produce high-quality images and is ideal for large runs, offering speed and efficiency. In contrast, letterpress printing employs a traditional technique where raised surfaces ink the substrate, suitable for smaller batches and providing a distinctive tactile quality. Both methods cater to diverse applications, enhancing branding and product identification through innovative label designs.</p></p>
