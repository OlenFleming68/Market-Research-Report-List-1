<p><p>Electric Double Layer Capacitors (EDLCs), also known as supercapacitors, are energy storage devices that store electrical energy through the electrostatic separation of charges. They consist of two conductive plates separated by an electrolyte, allowing them to achieve high capacitance values, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid energy discharge and charge cycles. EDLCs are increasingly used in various sectors, including automotive, consumer electronics, and renewable energy systems due to their ability to bridge the gap between conventional capacitors and batteries.</p><p>The Electric Double Layer Capacitor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
