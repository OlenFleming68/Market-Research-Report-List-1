<p><strong>The Anticoagulation Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NOACs</li><li>Warfarin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anticoagulation therapy includes two primary market types: Non-Vitamin K Oral Anticoagulants (NOACs) and Warfarin. NOACs, like rivaroxaban and apixaban, offer advantages such as fixed dosing and fewer dietary restrictions, making them increasingly popular for conditions like atrial fibrillation and venous thromboembolism. Conversely, Warfarin, a vitamin K antagonist, requires regular monitoring and adjustments, as it interacts with various foods and medications. The choice between these therapies depends on patient-specific factors, including efficacy, safety, and convenience.</p></p>
