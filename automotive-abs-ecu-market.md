<p><p>The Automotive ABS ECU market includes two primary types: ECUs with Conventional PCBs and ECUs with Hybrid PCBs. Conventional PCB-based ECUs use standard printed circuit boards, offering cost-effective solutions with reliable performance for basic ABS functionalities. Hybrid PCB-based ECUs integrate advanced materials and technologies, such as embedded sensors or flexible circuits, enhancing durability, efficiency, and adaptability for complex ABS systems. Both types cater to varying vehicle requirements, with hybrid PCBs targeting high-performance applications and conventional PCBs serving cost-sensitive markets.</p></p>
