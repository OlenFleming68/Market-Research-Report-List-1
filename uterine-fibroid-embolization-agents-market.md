<p><p>The uterine fibroid embolization agents market comprises various products used to block blood flow to fibroids. Gelatin sponge is a biodegradable agent that promotes clot formation. Polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) particles are non-biodegradable and create a permanent occlusion in blood vessels. Trisacryl gelatin microspheres (TAGM) are biocompatible and provide controlled particle size for effective occlusion. Polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA) microspheres are used for long-lasting embolization. Other agents may include various substances or new developments, contributing to diverse treatment options in this market.</p></p>
