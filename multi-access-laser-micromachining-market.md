<p><p>The Multi-Access Laser Micromachining market is segmented into two main types: Multi-Axis Low Power Micromachining and Multi-Axis High Power Micromachining. Multi-Axis Low Power Micromachining utilizes lower laser power for precise applications, such as delicate engravings or fine features on materials without causing thermal damage. In contrast, Multi-Axis High Power Micromachining involves higher laser power to efficiently process tougher materials, enabling rapid cutting, drilling, and material removal. Both types cater to diverse industries, including aerospace, electronics, and medical devices.</p></p>
