<p><p>The filtration papers market can be categorized into two main types: qualitative and quantitative filtration papers. Qualitative filtration papers are designed for general filtering applications, providing an efficient way to separate solids from liquids based on particle size and retention characteristics. In contrast, quantitative filtration papers are specifically engineered for precise analysis and measurement tasks, often used in laboratory settings to ensure complete retention of solids for accurate quantitative assessment. Both types serve essential roles in various scientific and industrial processes.</p></p>
