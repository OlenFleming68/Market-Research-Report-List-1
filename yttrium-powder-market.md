<p><p>Yttrium powder is categorized into three types based on purity levels: 3N (99.9% pure) and 4N (99.99% pure). The 3N grade is commonly used in applications such as ceramics and LED technology, providing good performance at a lower cost. Meanwhile, 4N grade yttrium powder offers higher purity, making it suitable for advanced applications like superconductors and precision optics where exceptional quality is crucial. The differentiation in purity dictates the market demand and pricing for these materials.</p></p>
