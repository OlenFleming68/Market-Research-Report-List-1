<p><p>Conveying tables are essential in material handling, categorized into three main types: roller conveying tables, belt conveying tables, and others. Roller conveying tables utilize a series of rollers to move products and are ideal for heavy items. Belt conveying tables employ a continuous belt for smooth and consistent transport, suitable for various product sizes. The "others" category includes unique systems like chain conveyors or magnetic tables, tailored for specific applications, enhancing efficiency in production and logistics processes.</p></p>
