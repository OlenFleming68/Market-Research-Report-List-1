<p><p>The automotive speed alert system market includes three key sensor types. Optical vehicle speed sensors use light beams to detect wheel rotation and speed. Hall-type vehicle speed sensors utilize magnetic fields to generate speed signals based on wheel movement. Magnetic-electric vehicle speed sensors rely on electromagnetic induction principles to measure speed changes. Each type offers unique advantages in accuracy and responsiveness, catering to different vehicle applications and enhancing driver safety by providing timely speed alerts.</p></p>
