<p><p>Immuno-oncology therapy encompasses several key market types. Monoclonal antibodies target specific antigens on cancer cells, enabling immune recognition and destruction. Immune checkpoint inhibitors block proteins that inhibit immune responses, revitalizing T-cell activity against tumors. Immune system modulators enhance overall immune function, helping the body to better fight cancer. Cancer vaccines stimulate the immune system to recognize and attack malignant cells. Together, these innovative approaches leverage the body’s own defenses to improve cancer treatment outcomes.</p></p>
