<p><p>The UAV mapping laser LiDAR market encompasses two primary types: Near-Infrared Laser Type and Water Penetrating Green Light Type. Near-Infrared laser systems are used for high-resolution terrain mapping, vegetation analysis, and topographic surveys, offering excellent penetration through foliage. In contrast, Water Penetrating Green Light lasers are designed to capture data underwater, making them ideal for mapping aquatic environments, submerged structures, and coastal zones. Both types enhance remote sensing capabilities, providing critical data for various applications in environmental monitoring, urban planning, and resource management.</p></p>
