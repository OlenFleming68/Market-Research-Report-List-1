<p><p>The Thermoplastic Polyurethane Rubber (TPU) market is primarily categorized into three types: polyester-based, polyether-based, and others. Polyester-based TPUs are known for their superior strength and abrasion resistance, making them suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Polyether-based TPUs offer excellent hydrolytic stability and flexibility, commonly used in footwear and medical devices. The "others" category encompasses specialized formulations, including flame-resistant or conductive TPUs that cater to niche applications, helping to diversify the market and meet specific customer needs.</p></p>
