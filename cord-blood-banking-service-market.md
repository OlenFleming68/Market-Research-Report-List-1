<p><p>Cord blood banking services are categorized into two main types: private and public. Private cord blood banking services allow families to store their newborn's cord blood for personal use, primarily for potential future medical treatments related to genetic disorders or diseases. In contrast, public cord blood banking services involve donating cord blood to a public registry, making it available for anyone in need of transplants, thus enhancing the overall availability of stem cells for research and treatment purposes.</p></p>
