Chairs with <2 degrees of freedom offer limited adjustments, typically for height and basic lumbar support. The 2-3 degrees of freedom chairs provide additional adjustments, like seat depth and armrest height, enhancing user comfort. Chairs with >3 degrees of freedom offer extensive customization, allowing adjustments for multiple angles and positions, catering to diverse ergonomic needs.</p></p>
