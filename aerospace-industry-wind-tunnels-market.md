<p><ul><li>Open Return Wind Tunnel</li><li>Closed Return Wind Tunnel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes two primary types of wind tunnels: open return wind tunnels and closed return wind tunnels. Open return wind tunnels intake air from the environment, allowing it to flow through the test section and exit back into the atmosphere. They are simpler and more cost-effective for certain applications. Closed return wind tunnels, on the other hand, recirculate air in a contained system, providing more consistent testing conditions and better control over environmental factors, making them ideal for high-precision tests.</p></p>
