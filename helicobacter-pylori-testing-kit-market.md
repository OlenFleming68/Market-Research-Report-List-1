<p><p>The Helicobacter Pylori testing kit market primarily features two types of diagnostic tests: antibody tests and antigen tests. Antibody tests detect the presence of antibodies against H. pylori in the blood, indicating past or current infection but may not specify active infection. In contrast, antigen tests identify H. pylori antigens in stool samples, providing a direct indication of current infection status. Both testing methods are crucial for diagnosing H. pylori-related conditions and guiding appropriate treatment decisions.</p></p>
