<p><p>Output couplers (OCs) are essential components in laser systems, facilitating the extraction of light while reflecting some back into the cavity. There are three primary market types: </p><p>1. **Plane OCs** offer uniform light distribution, commonly used in low-power applications.</p><p>2. **Concave OCs** focus light efficiently, enhancing output power and improving beam quality, ideal for high-performance lasers.</p><p>3. **Convex OCs** radiate light in a specific direction, often employed in applications requiring controlled beam divergence. Each type serves distinct operational needs in laser technology.</p></p>
