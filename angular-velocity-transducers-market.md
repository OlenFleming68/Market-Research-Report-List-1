<p><p>Angular velocity transducers measure the rate of rotation around an axis, and they are classified into different market types based on the number of axes they monitor. Single-axis transducers track rotation along one axis, making them suitable for simple applications. Dual-axis transducers monitor two axes, enabling more complex motion analysis. Three-axis transducers capture rotational motion in three dimensions, providing comprehensive data for advanced applications like robotics and aerospace. Each type caters to specific needs, ensuring versatile usage across various industries.</p></p>
