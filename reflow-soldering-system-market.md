<p><strong>The Reflow Soldering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Reflow Soldering</li><li>Condensation Reflow Soldering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Reflow Soldering System market includes two primary types: Convection Reflow Soldering and Condensation Reflow Soldering. Convection Reflow Soldering uses hot air to evenly distribute heat around the solder joints, ensuring effective melting and bonding. In contrast, Condensation Reflow Soldering relies on the condensation of vapor to transfer heat, benefiting from better temperature uniformity and reduced thermal stress. Both methods are crucial for efficiently soldering components in electronic manufacturing while meeting quality and reliability standards.</p></p>
