<p><p>Hybrid printing equipment encompasses various techniques that combine different printing processes for enhanced versatility. UV inkjet printing uses ultraviolet light to cure ink instantly, providing vibrant colors and durability. Water-based printing employs eco-friendly inks that are safe for indoor use. Solvent printing utilizes solvent-based inks for outdoor applications due to their weather resistance. LED curable printing offers energy efficiency and lower heat output compared to traditional methods. Dye sublimation transfers dye onto materials, ideal for textiles and promotional products, ensuring high-quality results.</p></p>
