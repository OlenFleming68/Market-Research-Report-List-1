<p><strong>The Waterproof Magnetic Reed Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Form A</li><li>Form B</li><li>Form C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof magnetic reed switch market is categorized into three types: Form A, Form B, and Form C. Form A switches close the circuit when a magnetic field is present and open when removed. Form B operates oppositely, opening the circuit in the presence of a magnetic field and closing when it is absent. Form C switches provide both functions, having a normally open and a normally closed contact. These variations cater to different application needs in harsh environments.</p></p>
