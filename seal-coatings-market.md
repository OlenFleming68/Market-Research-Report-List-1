<p><p>Seal coatings are classified into four main types: coal tar-based, asphalt-based, petroleum-based, and other types. </p><p>Coal tar-based sealers are known for their durability and resistance to oil and chemicals, making them popular for heavy-duty applications. Asphalt-based sealers, derived from asphalt, provide a balanced protection and are eco-friendlier. Petroleum-based sealers utilize refined petroleum products and offer effective protection but may have environmental concerns. Other types may include bio-based or specialty formulations catering to specific applications or environmental standards. Each type serves distinct needs in pavement maintenance.</p></p>
