<p><p>The CT contrast media market comprises various iodinated contrast agents, including Iohexol, Iodixanol, Iopamidol, and Ioversol. Iohexol is commonly used for various imaging procedures; it has a low viscosity and good patient tolerance. Iodixanol, a non-ionic agent, offers a higher osmolality and is preferred for specific applications. Iopamidol is known for its low allergic potential and is frequently utilized in cardiovascular imaging. Ioversol is used for its favorable safety profile and efficacy in enhancing diagnostic clarity across various imaging modalities.</p></p>
