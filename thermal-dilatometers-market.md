<p><strong>The Thermal Dilatometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitance Thermal Dilatometer</li><li>Connecting Rod (Push Rod) Thermal Dilatometer</li><li>Optical Thermal Dilatometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal dilatometers market consists of various types designed to measure thermal expansion in materials. Capacitance thermal dilatometers utilize changes in capacitance to assess dimensional changes with temperature variations. Connecting rod (push rod) thermal dilatometers employ a mechanical rod to translate thermal expansion into measurable movement. Optical thermal dilatometers leverage optical techniques, such as laser interferometry, to monitor precise changes in length as the material heats or cools. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on application requirements and sensitivity needed.</p></p>
