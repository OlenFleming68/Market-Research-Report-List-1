<p><ul><li>TFT Polarizer</li><li>TN Polarizer</li><li>STN Polarizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The display panel market features various polarizer types, crucial for enhancing image quality. TFT (Thin-Film Transistor) polarizers are used in high-resolution screens, providing superior contrast and color accuracy. TN (Twisted Nematic) polarizers are common in budget displays, offering faster response times but limited viewing angles and color reproduction. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) polarizers enhance TN technology, allowing better viewing angles and improved color performance, making them suitable for mid-range applications. Each type serves distinct market needs based on performance and cost.</p></p>
