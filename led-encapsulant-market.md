<p><p>The LED encapsulant market primarily comprises epoxy and silicone types, each serving distinct applications. Epoxy encapsulants are known for their excellent adhesion, strength, and thermal conductivity, making them suitable for high-performance LED applications. They cure quickly, providing robust protection against environmental factors. On the other hand, silicone encapsulants offer superior flexibility, thermal stability, and moisture resistance, ideal for outdoor and extreme temperature environments. Both types enhance the durability and efficiency of LED products while addressing specific performance requirements.</p></p>
