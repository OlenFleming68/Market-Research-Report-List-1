<p><p>Cold Laser Therapy devices are categorized into two main types: Continuous Laser and Pulse Laser. Continuous Laser devices emit a constant beam of light, promoting consistent energy delivery to the targeted tissues, which is effective for deeper penetration and sustained therapeutic effects. In contrast, Pulse Laser devices deliver energy in short bursts, allowing for increased tissue reaction and promoting healing processes by stimulating cellular activity. Both types are utilized for pain relief, tissue repair, and inflammation reduction in various clinical settings.</p></p>
