<p><p>The MEP software market encompasses two primary types: BIM (Building Information Modeling) MEP software and CAD (Computer-Aided Design) MEP software. BIM MEP software focuses on creating and managing 3D digital representations of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems, enabling enhanced collaboration and efficiency throughout the building lifecycle. In contrast, CAD MEP software primarily utilizes 2D and 3D drafting tools to design and document MEP systems, facilitating precise layout and coordination. Both types play crucial roles in improving project outcomes in the construction industry.</p></p>
