<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RTD PT100 sensors are temperature sensors with a resistance of 100 ohms at 0°C, widely used in industrial applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: wire-wound RTDs and thin film RTDs. Wire-wound RTDs are made from coiled metal wire, offering high accuracy and stability, making them suitable for extreme conditions. In contrast, thin film RTDs utilize a deposited metal film on a ceramic substrate, providing compactness, quicker response times, and cost-effectiveness, ideal for less demanding applications.</p></p>
