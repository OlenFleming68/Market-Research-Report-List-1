<p><strong>The Automotive Chassis Dynamometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Roller</li><li>Multi Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive chassis dynamometer market is segmented into single roller and multi-roller types. Single roller dynamometers typically feature a single cylindrical roller that accommodates lighter vehicles, providing basic performance testing and emissions measurement. In contrast, multi-roller dynamometers consist of multiple rollers, allowing for more comprehensive testing of various vehicle types, including larger and heavier models. They enable detailed simulation of road conditions and dynamic vehicle behavior, making them ideal for advanced diagnostics, performance tuning, and regulatory compliance tasks within the automotive sector.</p></p>
