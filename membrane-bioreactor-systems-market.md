<p><ul><li>Separate MBR</li><li>Integral MBR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) systems are categorized into two main market types: Separate MBR and Integral MBR. Separate MBR systems consist of a dedicated membrane unit that operates independently from the biological treatment process, allowing for greater flexibility in design and operation. In contrast, Integral MBR systems integrate the membrane filtration process directly within the bioreactor, streamlining operation and reducing footprint. Both types are widely used in wastewater treatment, offering efficient solid-liquid separation and improved effluent quality.</p></p>
