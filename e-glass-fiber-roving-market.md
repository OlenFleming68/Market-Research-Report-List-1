<p><ul><li>Single-end Roving</li><li>Multi-end Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E Glass fiber roving is categorized into two main types: single-end roving and multi-end roving. Single-end roving consists of a single strand of glass fibers, offering high flexibility and ease of handling, making it ideal for small-scale applications. Multi-end roving, on the other hand, comprises multiple strands bundled together, providing greater strength and consistency, suitable for larger applications like composites and industrial parts. Both types are essential in manufacturing, reinforcing materials, and enhancing product performance.</p></p>
