<p><strong>The Boat Trim System (BTS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boat Trim System (BTS) enhances vessel performance by adjusting its angle relative to the water, optimizing speed and stability. The market comprises two main types: trim tabs and interceptors. Trim tabs are adjustable surfaces mounted on a boat's transom, allowing for fine-tuning of the hull's position. Interceptors, on the other hand, are fixed devices that provide lift and improve handling by altering the water flow around the hull, enhancing overall maneuverability and efficiency.</p></p>
