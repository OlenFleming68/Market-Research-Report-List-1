<p><p>The hair dye raw materials market is categorized into two main types: liquid and solid raw materials. Liquid raw materials typically include ammonia, hydrogen peroxide, and various dyes that dissolve easily, allowing for smooth application and even color distribution. Solid raw materials, on the other hand, consist of powders and granules, such as color pigments and thickeners, which require mixing with a liquid medium before use. Both categories play crucial roles in the formulation and effectiveness of hair dye products.</p></p>
