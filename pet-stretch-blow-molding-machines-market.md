<p><ul><li>Single Step PET Stretch Blow Molding Machines</li><li>Two Step PET Stretch Blow Molding Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET stretch blow molding machines market is categorized into two main types: single-step and two-step machines. Single-step PET stretch blow molding machines combine the bottle formation and preform manufacturing processes in one cycle, making them efficient for small to medium production runs. Conversely, two-step machines separate these processes, allowing for larger production volumes and greater flexibility in preform designs. Each type serves different market needs, with single-step machines optimal for quick runs and two-step machines ideal for high output.</p></p>
