<p><p>The industrial water chillers market comprises several types, each suited to specific applications. Screw chillers use rotor technology for efficient and reliable cooling, ideal for large facilities. Centrifugal chillers are known for their high capacity and energy efficiency, making them suitable for large-scale operations. Reciprocating chillers utilize piston-driven compressors, delivering precise cooling for various industrial needs. Additionally, other chillers, such as absorption chillers and gas-fired chillers, cater to niche markets, expanding the overall market's versatility and application range.</p></p>
