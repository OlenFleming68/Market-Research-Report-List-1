<p><p>Text message marketing systems are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based systems operate on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from various devices without the need for local installations. This model allows businesses to manage campaigns effortlessly while benefiting from automatic updates. Conversely, on-premises systems are installed directly on a company’s infrastructure, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Both types cater to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
