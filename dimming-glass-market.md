<p><p>The dimming glass market includes various types, primarily categorized into PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) glass, electrochromic glass, and other specialized technologies. PDLC glass utilizes liquid crystal polymers that transition between transparent and opaque when voltage is applied, providing privacy and glare reduction. Electrochromic glass changes its tint in response to electrical charge, allowing for energy efficiency and light control. Other technologies may include suspended particle devices or thermochromic materials, catering to specific applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
