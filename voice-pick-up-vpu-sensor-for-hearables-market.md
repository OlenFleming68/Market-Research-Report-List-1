<p><p>Voice Pick Up (VPU) sensors in the hearables market include piezoresistive pressure sensors and piezoelectric sensors. Piezoresistive sensors detect changes in resistance when pressure is applied, offering precise voice capture in various environments. Piezoelectric sensors generate an electrical charge in response to mechanical stress, effectively picking up sound vibrations. Both types enhance audio performance, enabling clearer voice communication and noise cancellation in hearable devices, catering to the growing demand for advanced auditory experiences in personal audio technology.</p></p>
