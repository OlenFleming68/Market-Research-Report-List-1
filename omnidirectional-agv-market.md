<p><p>Omnidirectional Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) are designed to navigate in any direction, enhancing efficiency in material handling. There are primarily two market types for guiding these AGVs: optical guiding and magnetic guiding. Optical guiding utilizes cameras and sensors to detect markers or reflective tapes on the floor, allowing precise navigation. In contrast, magnetic guiding relies on embedded magnetic strips or sensors to navigate predefined paths, ensuring consistent and reliable maneuvering in dynamic environments. Both systems cater to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
