<p><p>The bio-derived enzymes for detergents market includes various types such as amylases, cellulases, lipases, peroxidases, and pullulanases. Amylases break down starches, enhancing stain removal from fabrics. Cellulases soften fabrics and improve color brightness by breaking down cellulose. Lipases target fats and oils, effectively cleaning greasy stains. Peroxidases aid in stain removal and whitening fabrics through oxidation, while pullulanases further enhance starch degradation, improving detergent efficiency. Together, these enzymes optimize performance in laundry and dishwashing products, promoting eco-friendliness.</p></p>
