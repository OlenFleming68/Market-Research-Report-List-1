<p><p>Resorbable dental membranes are biocompatible materials used in periodontal and dental implant procedures to promote tissue regeneration. They provide a barrier that supports the regeneration of bone and soft tissue while gradually breaking down and being absorbed by the body, eliminating the need for a second surgical procedure to remove non-resorbable membranes. The materials commonly used for these membranes include collagen and synthetic polymers, which facilitate healing and encourage optimal tissue integration.</p><p>The Resorbable Dental Membrane Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.20% during the forecast period. <p><p>The resorbable dental membrane market primarily features two types: cowhide and pigskin membranes. Cowhide membranes are derived from bovine sources and are widely used for their biocompatibility and effective barrier properties in guided tissue regeneration. Pigskin membranes, sourced from porcine tissues, offer similar benefits and are known for their superior handling characteristics and consistent thickness. Both types serve as scaffolds to support tissue healing, making them essential in dental procedures like implant placement and periodontal surgery.</p></p>
