<p><p>The pneumatic lithotripter market comprises two main types: Pneumatic Lithotripters without Ultrasonic and Ultrasonic Pneumatic Lithotripters. Pneumatic Lithotripters without Ultrasonic utilize a high-pressure air mechanism to break down kidney stones without additional ultrasonic assistance, focusing on mechanical shock waves. In contrast, Ultrasonic Pneumatic Lithotripters combine air pressure with ultrasonic energy, enhancing stone fragmentation efficiency and improving patient outcomes. Both types are integral in urological procedures, offering options tailored to various clinical needs.</p></p>
