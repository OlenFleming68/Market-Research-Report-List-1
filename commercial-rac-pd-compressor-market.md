<p><strong>The Commercial RAC PD Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Type</li><li>Scroll Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial RAC (Room Air Conditioning) PD (Positive Displacement) compressor market is primarily categorized into two types: reciprocating and scroll compressors. Reciprocating compressors use pistons driven by a crankshaft to compress refrigerant, offering high efficiency and reliability for various applications. In contrast, scroll compressors consist of two spirals where one orbits the other, providing smooth operation with reduced noise and vibration. Both types cater to different market needs, balancing performance, energy efficiency, and space considerations in commercial settings.</p></p>
