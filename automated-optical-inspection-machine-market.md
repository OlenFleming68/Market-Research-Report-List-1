<p><p>The Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) machine market consists of two primary types: online and offline AOI machines. Online AOI machines are integrated into the production line, providing real-time inspection of products as they are manufactured. This allows for immediate feedback and corrective actions. In contrast, offline AOI machines operate outside of the production process, where completed products are inspected for defects after manufacturing. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring product quality and minimizing defects in various industries.</p></p>
