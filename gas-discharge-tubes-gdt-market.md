<p><p>Gas Discharge Tubes (GDTs) are protective devices used in electronic circuits to safeguard sensitive equipment from voltage spikes and surges. Composed of a sealed glass tube filled with a specific gas, GDTs conduct electricity during over-voltage situations, redirecting the excess energy away from critical components. This functionality is crucial in various applications, including telecommunications, power systems, and consumer electronics, where reliable protection against transient voltages is essential.</p><p>The Gas Discharge Tubes (GDT) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.3% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for robust electronic protection solutions across multiple industries.
