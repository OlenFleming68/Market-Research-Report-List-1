<p><p>The wooden biomass power generation market encompasses various technologies for converting wood into energy. Combustion technology involves directly burning wooden biomass to produce heat for electricity generation, while co-firing integrates biomass with fossil fuels in existing power plants to enhance sustainability. Cogeneration, or combined heat and power (CHP), simultaneously generates electricity and useful thermal energy from biomass, increasing overall efficiency. These methods leverage renewable resources, contributing to cleaner energy solutions and reduced greenhouse gas emissions in the power generation sector.</p></p>
