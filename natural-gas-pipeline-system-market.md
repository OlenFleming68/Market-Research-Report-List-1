<p><strong>The Natural Gas Pipeline System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gathering Pipelines</li><li>Transportation Pipelines</li><li>Distribution Pipelines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural gas pipeline system comprises three main market types: gathering, transportation, and distribution pipelines. Gathering pipelines collect gas from production sites and transport it to processing facilities. Transportation pipelines, which are larger in diameter and capacity, move processed natural gas over long distances to demand centers or storage facilities. Finally, distribution pipelines deliver gas from local transmission systems directly to consumers, including homes and businesses, ensuring a reliable supply for everyday use.</p></p>
