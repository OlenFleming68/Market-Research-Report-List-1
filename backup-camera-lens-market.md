<p><p>The Backup Camera Lens Market includes CCD and CMOS cameras, both used for rearview imaging in vehicles. CCD cameras offer high image quality with better light sensitivity, ideal for low-light conditions, but are costlier and consume more power. CMOS cameras are more affordable, energy-efficient, and compact, making them popular for modern vehicles, though they may have slightly lower image quality in certain conditions. Both types enhance safety by providing clear rearview visuals, with CMOS dominating due to cost and efficiency advantages.</p></p>
