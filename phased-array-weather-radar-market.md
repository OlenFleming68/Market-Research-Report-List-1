<p><p>Phased Array Weather Radar markets are categorized into dual polarized and fully polarized systems. Dual polarized radars transmit and receive signals in both horizontal and vertical orientations, enhancing precipitation estimation and hydrometeor classification. Fully polarized radars, on the other hand, utilize advanced techniques to analyze the full polarization state of electromagnetic waves, providing in-depth insights into weather phenomena. Both types enhance atmospheric observation, resulting in improved forecasting accuracy and better understanding of severe weather patterns.</p></p>
