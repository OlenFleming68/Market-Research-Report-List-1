<p><p>The aluminum flux market is segmented into resin-based and water-soluble types. Resin-based fluxes are designed for use in high-temperature applications and provide excellent bonding properties, making them suitable for various aluminum welding and fabrication processes. In contrast, water-soluble fluxes are used for their ease of cleaning and low environmental impact, making them ideal for applications where residue removal is critical. Each type caters to specific industry demands, influencing their adoption in manufacturing and metallurgical operations.</p></p>
