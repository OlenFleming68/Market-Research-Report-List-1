<p><strong>The Conductivity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Conductivity Sensor</li><li>Electrodeless Conductivity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductivity sensors market comprises various types, notably inductive and electrodeless sensors. Inductive conductivity sensors operate without direct contact with the liquid, using electromagnetic fields to measure conductivity, making them ideal for corrosive or contaminated fluids. Electrodeless conductivity sensors, on the other hand, utilize capacitive principles to assess conductivity without electrodes, providing enhanced durability and reliability. Both types are widely used in water treatment, chemical processing, and environmental monitoring, ensuring accurate measurements in diverse applications.</p></p>
