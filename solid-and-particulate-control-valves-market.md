<p><p>The Solid and Particulate Control Valves market encompasses various types, including ball valves, gate valves, knife valves, and others. Ball valves provide quick shut-off and are ideal for throttling, while gate valves ensure minimal pressure drop, suitable for on/off applications. Knife valves excel in handling slurries and thick materials due to their sharp-edged blades that cut through solids. Other valve types may include globe and check valves, each serving specific functions in regulating flow in industrial processes.</p></p>
