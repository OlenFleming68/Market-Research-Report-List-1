<p><strong>The Small Size Panel Display Driver IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Source Drive IC</li><li>Gate Drive IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Small Size Panel Display Driver IC market is categorized primarily into Source Drive ICs and Gate Drive ICs. Source Drive ICs are responsible for controlling pixel brightness and colors, directly managing the rows of pixels in displays like smartphones and tablets. In contrast, Gate Drive ICs control the timing of the signal applied to the display's columns, enabling effective refresh rates and image stability. Both types are essential for enhancing performance, efficiency, and image quality in small-sized display panels.</p></p>
