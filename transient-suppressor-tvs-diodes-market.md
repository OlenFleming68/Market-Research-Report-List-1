<p><p>The Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) Diodes market is segmented into two main types: Uni-Polar and Bi-Polar TVS diodes. Uni-Polar TVS diodes are designed to protect circuits from voltage spikes in one direction, commonly used in applications with a standard polarity. In contrast, Bi-Polar TVS diodes can clamp transient voltages in both directions, making them suitable for circuits that require bidirectional protection, such as AC applications. This differentiation helps in addressing the specific needs of diverse electronic applications.</p></p>
