<p><strong>The ECHO Cardiography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>M-mode</li><li>Two-dimensional (2-D, B-mode or real time)</li><li>Doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECHO Cardiography encompasses various types, each serving unique diagnostic purposes. M-mode provides a single-dimensional view, allowing precise measurement of cardiac structures over time. Two-dimensional (2-D) ECHO, also known as B-mode or real-time imaging, offers a comprehensive view of the heart's anatomy and function. Doppler ECHO assesses blood flow dynamics by measuring the speed and direction of blood movement. Together, these techniques enhance cardiac imaging and enable accurate diagnosis and monitoring of cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
