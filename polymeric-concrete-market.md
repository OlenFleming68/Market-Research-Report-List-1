<p><p>The polymeric concrete market comprises three main types: aggregates, synthetic resins, and other additives. Aggregates are crucial for providing bulk and structural integrity, typically including sand, gravel, and crushed stone. Synthetic resins enhance adhesion and durability, improving the overall performance and flexibility of the concrete. Other additives, such as curing agents and accelerators, further optimize properties like workability, strength, and resistance to environmental factors. Together, these components contribute to the versatility and functionality of polymeric concrete in construction applications.</p></p>
