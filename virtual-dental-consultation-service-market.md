<p><p>The Virtual Dental Consultation Service market comprises two primary types: synchronous and asynchronous consultation services. Synchronous consultation allows real-time interactions between patients and dental professionals, typically through video calls, enabling immediate feedback and assessments. Conversely, asynchronous consultation involves communication that is not simultaneous, where patients can send inquiries, images, or videos, and receive responses from dentists later. Both services enhance accessibility and convenience, catering to different patient needs and preferences in the evolving landscape of dental care.</p></p>
