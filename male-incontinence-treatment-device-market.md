<p><p>The male incontinence treatment device market primarily includes male slings and artificial urinary sphincters. Male slings are minimally invasive devices designed to support the urethra and prevent involuntary leakage of urine, offering a surgical option for mild to moderate incontinence. In contrast, artificial urinary sphincters are implanted devices that mimic the natural function of the sphincter, allowing men with severe incontinence to regain control over urination. Both solutions aim to enhance quality of life for individuals suffering from male incontinence.</p></p>
