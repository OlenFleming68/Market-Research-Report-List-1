<p><strong>The Automotive Composite Leaf Springs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transversal</li><li>Longitudinal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive composite leaf springs market includes two main types: transversal and longitudinal. Transversal leaf springs are mounted horizontally across the vehicle's width, providing lateral stability and compact design, often used in lighter vehicles. Longitudinal leaf springs are mounted along the vehicle's length, offering durability and load-bearing capacity, commonly found in trucks and heavy-duty vehicles. Both types utilize composite materials like fiberglass or carbon fiber for reduced weight, improved performance, and enhanced fuel efficiency compared to traditional steel springs.</p></p>
