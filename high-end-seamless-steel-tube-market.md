<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Seamless Steel Tube</li><li>Cold Rolled Seamless Steel Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end seamless steel tube market is divided into two primary types: hot rolled and cold rolled seamless steel tubes. Hot rolled seamless steel tubes are produced by extrusion and are characterized by their high strength and durability, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, cold rolled seamless steel tubes are processed at room temperature, resulting in superior dimensional accuracy and surface finish. Both types cater to industries such as construction, automotive, and energy, where performance and reliability are crucial.</p></p>
