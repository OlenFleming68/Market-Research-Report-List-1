<p><p>The 32-channel EEG system market is categorized into three main types: Conventional EEG, Video EEG, and Dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG focuses on capturing brain electrical activity, primarily for diagnosis and monitoring. Video EEG combines EEG data with video recordings to analyze seizure activity and related behaviors. Dynamic EEG involves advanced techniques for continuous monitoring, often adapting to patient movements, offering insights into brain function over time. Each type serves specific clinical needs in neurology and mental health.</p></p>
