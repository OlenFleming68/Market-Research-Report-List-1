<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Assist Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Column EPS</li><li>Pinion EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Electric Power Assist (EPS) market includes Column EPS and Pinion EPS systems. Column EPS integrates the motor and control unit on the steering column, offering a compact design suitable for smaller vehicles. It provides moderate assistance and is cost-effective. Pinion EPS places the motor and control unit on the steering rack, delivering higher torque and precision, making it ideal for larger vehicles. Both systems enhance steering efficiency, reduce driver effort, and improve fuel economy by replacing traditional hydraulic systems with electric assistance.</p></p>
