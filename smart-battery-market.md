<p><p>The smart battery market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Lead Acid** batteries are cost-effective and widely used in automotive applications, offering reliable performance.</p><p>2. **Nickel Cadmium (NiCd)** batteries, known for their durability, perform well in extreme conditions but suffer from memory effect.</p><p>3. **Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH)** batteries provide higher capacities and are more environmentally friendly than NiCd.</p><p>4. **Lithium Ion** batteries are lightweight, with high energy density, suitable for portable electronics.</p><p>5. **Lithium Ion Polymer** batteries are flexible in shape and design, offering enhanced safety and performance for modern devices.</p></p>
