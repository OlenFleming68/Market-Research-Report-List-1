<p><p>The eyeglass lenses market is primarily categorized into three types: glass, resin, and others. Resin lenses, made from lightweight plastic materials, are popular for their impact resistance and comfort, making them ideal for everyday wear. The "other" category includes specialty materials like polycarbonate and high-index options, catering to specific needs such as thinness or enhanced visual performance.</p></p>
