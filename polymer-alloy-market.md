<p><ul><li>PC-based Alloys</li><li>PPO/PPE-based Alloys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polymer Alloy market is divided primarily into PC-based alloys and PPO/PPE-based alloys. PC-based alloys are known for their high impact resistance, transparency, and thermal stability, making them suitable for applications in automotive, electronics, and construction. Conversely, PPO/PPE-based alloys exhibit excellent flame resistance, dimensional stability, and chemical resistance, ideal for electrical and industrial applications. Both types contribute to enhanced performance and diversified applications across various industries, driven by evolving consumer demands and technological advancements.</p></p>
