<p><ul><li>Active System</li><li>Passive System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial solar water heater market comprises two main types: active and passive systems. Active systems utilize electric pumps and controllers to circulate water, offering enhanced efficiency and temperature control, making them suitable for large-scale applications. Conversely, passive systems rely on natural convection and gravity, making them simpler and less costly to install, ideal for smaller or retrofitted commercial settings. Both systems harness solar energy to reduce water heating costs and contribute to sustainability efforts.</p></p>
