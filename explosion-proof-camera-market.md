<p><ul><li>Integrated Explosion Proof Camera</li><li>Split Type Explosion Proof Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The explosion-proof camera market features two main types: integrated and split-type cameras. Integrated explosion-proof cameras combine all components, including the lens, sensor, and housing, into a single unit, offering a compact solution ideal for various hazardous environments. Conversely, split-type explosion-proof cameras consist of separate components, with the sensor located remotely from the housing. This design allows for flexible installations and easier maintenance, making them suitable for larger or complex setups in industries like oil and gas, chemicals, and mining.</p></p>
