<p><p>The Zero Delay Clock Buffer market is categorized into two main types: differential and single-ended. Differential buffers use two signals that are opposite in phase, enhancing noise immunity and minimizing signal distortion, making them ideal for high-speed applications. In contrast, single-ended buffers utilize a single signal pathway, which simplifies design and is typically easier to implement but may be more susceptible to noise. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining signal integrity across various electronic systems.</p></p>
