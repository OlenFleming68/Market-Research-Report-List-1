<p><strong>The Optical Transport Network Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WDM</li><li>DWDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Transport Network market comprises various technologies, primarily Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing (DWDM). WDM allows multiple light wavelengths to transmit simultaneously over a single optical fiber, boosting bandwidth and efficiency. DWDM enhances this by utilizing closely spaced wavelengths, enabling even greater data transmission capacity and supporting longer distances without signal degradation. Both technologies are essential for meeting increasing data demands in telecommunications and data center connectivity.</p></p>
