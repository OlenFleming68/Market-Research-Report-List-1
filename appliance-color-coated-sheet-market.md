<p><p>The Appliance Color-Coated Sheet market primarily consists of two types: PCM (Pre-Coated Metal) and VCM (Vinyl Coated Metal) sheets. PCM sheets are coated with a polymer to enhance durability and aesthetic appeal, making them suitable for household appliances. In contrast, VCM sheets feature a vinyl coating that provides excellent resistance to moisture and corrosion, ideal for kitchen and home appliances. Both types enhance product longevity and design flexibility, catering to varied consumer preferences in the appliance sector.</p></p>
