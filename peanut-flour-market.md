<p><p>Peanut flour is classified into three main types based on roasting levels: light, medium, and dark. Light peanut flour is made from lightly roasted peanuts, offering a mild flavor and bright color, ideal for baking and smoothies. Medium peanut flour has a balanced taste and is versatile for various recipes, including sauces and snacks. Dark peanut flour, produced from more heavily roasted peanuts, delivers a rich, pronounced flavor, making it suitable for savory dishes and gourmet applications.</p></p>
