<p><strong>The Nasal Drug Delivery Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized Type</li><li>Non-pressurized Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nasal drug delivery technology is categorized into two main types: pressurized and non-pressurized. Pressurized systems utilize aerosol canisters to deliver drugs in a fine mist, enhancing absorption and providing consistent dosing. In contrast, non-pressurized systems, such as nasal sprays and drops, rely on gravity or manual pump mechanisms for delivery, allowing for easier administration and patient control. Both types offer advantages in terms of rapid onset of action and improved bioavailability compared to traditional oral routes.</p></p>
