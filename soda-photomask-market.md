<p><strong>The Soda Photomask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soda Chrome Mask</li><li>Soda Emulsion Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Soda Photomask Market is divided mainly into two types: Soda Chrome Masks and Soda Emulsion Masks. Soda Chrome Masks utilize a photolithographic process featuring a layer of chromium on a glass substrate, offering high precision and durability, ideal for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, Soda Emulsion Masks leverage a photosensitive emulsion to create patterns, providing flexibility for various applications, including circuit boards and display technologies. Both mask types play crucial roles in achieving high-resolution patterns in electronic devices.</p></p>
