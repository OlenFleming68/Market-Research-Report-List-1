<p><p>A chlorophyll meter is a portable device used to measure the chlorophyll content in plants, which is an indicator of plant health and nitrogen levels. It helps farmers and researchers optimize fertilizer application, improve crop yield, and reduce environmental impact by providing real-time data on plant nutrient status. The device works by emitting light at specific wavelengths and measuring the absorption and reflection of light by the plant's leaves.</p><p>The Chlorophyll Meter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period.
