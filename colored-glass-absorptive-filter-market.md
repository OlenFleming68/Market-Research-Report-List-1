<p><strong>The Colored Glass Absorptive Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longpass</li><li>Shortpass</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colored glass absorptive filter market is categorized into three main types: longpass filters, shortpass filters, and others. Longpass filters allow wavelengths longer than a specified cutoff to pass through while absorbing shorter wavelengths, making them ideal for enhancing specific color ranges. Shortpass filters, conversely, permit shorter wavelengths to pass while blocking longer ones, useful for applications needing selective color filtering. The "others" category includes specialized filters with unique characteristics tailored to specific industrial, scientific, or artistic needs.</p></p>
